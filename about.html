Hello! Hi!

I'm Alicia, a UX designer who dabbles in front-end development. I graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a degree in Human-Computer Interaction and Linguistics.

I have a passion for puzzle solving and creating user-friendly technology. I am driven by curiosity and the need to understand the needs and motivations of human behavior. My goal as a designer is to create inclusive and intuitive designs to improve the lives of users in an efficient and collaborative manner.
